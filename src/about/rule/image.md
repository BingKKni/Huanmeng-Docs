- 在账号简介或其他显著位置明确声明禁止转载或使用其插画的；
- 在大量插画作品中的明显位置标注禁止转载或使用的。

- 在账号简介或其他显著位置注明禁止未授权转载或使用插画的；

以上判断均以图片收集当时的信息为准。若老师后续更新了插画作品的使用条款（例如此前允许使用，但后面禁止了），由于我们难以持续追踪每一位老师对自己作品的最新声明，恕无法对更新前的使用行为承担责任，非常抱歉。但一旦我们获悉老师的最新要求，会立即依据其指示对已使用的图片进行相应处理（包括但不限于下架、补充标注等）。

### English:
We deeply acknowledge and highly value the legal rights that each illustrator (hereafter referred to as "the artist") holds over their work. Our primary principle is to always respect the artist's copyright statement before collecting and using any illustration. If an artist indicates in their profile bio or another prominent location that authorization is required, we will generally contact them beforehand to obtain permission. We will also strictly adhere to any other requests the artist may have (e.g., crediting their Twitter ID).
**We will NOT collect images from the following types of artists:**
- Those who have explicitly stated in their profile bio or another prominent location that reprinting or use of their images is prohibited.
- Those who have clearly marked on a significant number of their works that reprinting or use is prohibited.

**In the following situations, we will generally take the initiative to contact artists for permission to use their work:**
- Artists who have stated in their profile bio or another prominent location that unauthorized reprinting or use of their images is prohibited.
- Artists who provide a valid Bilibili, miHoYo BBS/HoYoLab, QQ, or mainland Chinese email address in their profile bio or another prominent location.
All the above judgments are based on the information available at the time the image was collected. If an artist subsequently updates the terms of use for their illustrations (e.g., previously permitted use but later prohibited it), we regret that we cannot be held responsible for usage prior to the update, as it is difficult for us to continuously track the latest statements from every artist. We sincerely apologize for this. However, once we become aware of an artist's new requirements, we will immediately take appropriate action regarding the used images in accordance with their instructions (including but not limited to removal, adding credit, etc.).
We solemnly promise and sincerely welcome artists and their fans to supervise and provide feedback via the following email address. If you find that our use of an image violates your statement, please contact us promptly.
Furthermore, we collect images solely for the purpose of free sharing and for users to enjoy. Our bot is entirely non-profit, free of charge, and all its functions are available to all users unconditionally. We pledge not to use any of the works for commercial purposes.
If you believe an image we are using infringes upon your rights, please contact us at the following email address (Email: xbudingqwq@gmail.com; we recommend attaching the relevant image to help us locate it quickly). Upon receiving your email, we will cooperate unconditionally and delete the image immediately. We mean no offense and sincerely ask for the artist's understanding.
